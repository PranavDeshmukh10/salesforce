## Is Salesforce a CRM?
- Yes, Salesforce is a CRM. In fact, Salesforce is the #1 CRM in the industry.
- It is based on two important aspects which are Cloud Based Architecture & Pay As You Go model.

## What is this Cloud Based Architecture or Cloud Computing?
- Cloud Computing is the transformation of storing data online rather than offline in your local repository or environment.
- Cloud computing offers different computing models, which are below
	- IaaS (Infrastructure as a Service)
	- PaaS (Platform as a Service)
	- SaaS (Software as a Service)

## What is force.com?
The platform that provides Salesforce to be run & used by business is force.com  

## Where does Salesforce stands in Cloud Computing?
- Salesforce comes under SaaS model. Where all the services, platform are managed by the organization and you just have to use it paying the required amount.
- Whereas force.com comes under PaaS.

## Advantages of Salesforce?
- Managed from a central location
- Hosted remotely
- Easily accessible over the internet
- Pay as you go model
- No installations required.
